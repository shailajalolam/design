Applicants must read the following requirements and instructions before compketing this form.


1. The applicant must fill the load form in full, incomplete forms will be rejected
2. Loans are granted in accordance with loaning policy and lending conditions existing at the time of application.

12. Any alteration on the loan from must be countersigned/No change of loan type or period.
